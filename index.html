Specialized Child & Family Therapy

With 19 years of experience, I provide professional therapy services designed specifically for children, teens, and families in the Louisville, Kentucky area. Children require different approaches than adults—and that's exactly what I offer.

Areas of Focus

I specialize in challenges that require specific expertise and child-centered interventions.

Child Therapy

Specialized interventions designed from the ground up for how children process, communicate, and heal—not just adapted adult techniques.

Trauma & PTSD

Evidence-based trauma treatment including EMDR, helping children and families recover from difficult experiences.

Parenting Support

Empowering parents to understand their child's needs and take back control of family dynamics with confidence.

Childhood Chronic Constipation

One of the few therapists in Louisville specializing in treating chronic constipation (encopresis) in children—a condition often linked to behavioral and emotional factors.

Why Child Therapy is Different

Children aren't small adults. They think, feel, and communicate differently.

Play-Based Methods

Children express themselves through play. I use therapeutic play, sandtray, and art to meet them where they are.

Family Involvement

Parents are partners in the process. I provide immediate, actionable guidance—not just observation from the sidelines.

Direct Approach

I'm often the therapist parents find after trying others with a less direct approach. I give you real answers and real strategies.

Common questions about therapy services in Louisville, KY

Getting Started

The easiest way is through my online intake form, which you can access from the Contact page. You can also call me at (502) 912-8889. I offer free initial consultations so we can discuss your family's needs before committing to ongoing therapy.

Yes, I am currently accepting new clients. I see children, teens, and families in the Louisville, Kentucky area, including Jeffersontown, St. Matthews, Middletown, and surrounding communities.

The first session is an opportunity for us to get to know each other. I'll ask about your concerns, your child's history, and what you're hoping to achieve through therapy.

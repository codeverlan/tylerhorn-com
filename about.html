About Me

19 years dedicated to helping children and families

Tyler Horn, MSSW, LCSW

Licensed Clinical Social Worker Kentucky License #3476 19 Years Experience

A Qualified Therapist for Help with Uncommon Problems.

If you have a concern about your child, I know it can keep you up at night. Maybe you've tried internet searches or self-help books—but lately, you're feeling ready for some professional help.

I take pride in meeting your family where you are with professional, helpful, and immediate suggestions. I am a great fit for families who feel they have not been asked to participate in their child's therapy very often.

Some clients view me as unique because of my straightforward approach. I'm often the person parents find after they have tried professionals with a less direct approach.

My Philosophy

Parents Back in Charge

There are many reasons why things aren't quite as they should be in a household. I provide parents and caregivers with the opportunity to set things right. I believe that understanding the core issues with your child, yourself, and within your family is the key to lasting change.

Children Require Different Approaches

Children aren't small adults—they think, communicate, and heal differently. The interventions I use for children are designed specifically for how kids process the world. Play therapy and sandtray aren't just "fun versions" of talk therapy; they're evidence-based approaches that work because they meet children where they are developmentally.

Direct and Honest

I offer trauma, attachment, and person-centered care based on almost 20 years of full-time work with adults, children, teens, and families. You won't leave our sessions wondering what you should do next—I give immediate, actionable guidance that you can put into practice right away.
